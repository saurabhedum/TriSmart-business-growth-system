🔍 Revenue Leakage Prevention Making invisible leakage of 8–12% revenue visible Research · 3 Workflows ▼
R1Real-Time Margin Monitoring Per Transaction▼
📋 About

Every bill is checked against purchase cost at billing time. If a discount drops margin below a set floor, a warning triggers before the bill is saved. In India's electronics retail, 73% of margin erosion happens at the billing stage through unauthorised discounts.

⚙️ Logic & Flow
Discount entered by salesman→System calculates net margin→Compares with floor %→If below → warning shown→Requires manager approval to proceed
🧩 Key Elements
Margin Floor ConfigReal-Time CheckWarning ModalManager Approval Flow
⚡ Gap in Standard ERPs

Most ERPs show margins in monthly reports. By then the damage is done. This requires real-time flagging at point of sale — absent from standard systems.

R2GST Input Credit Mismatch Alert▼
📋 About

Compares your purchase records with your supplier's GSTR-2A filings. If a supplier hasn't filed their returns, you cannot claim input credit. Retailers lose an average of ₹18,000–₹45,000 per year in unclaimed ITC due to supplier non-compliance.

⚙️ Logic & Flow
Purchase bills logged→GSTR-2A pulled from GSTN API→Match by supplier GSTIN + invoice→Mismatches flagged→ITC at risk amount shown
🧩 Key Elements
GSTN API IntegrationGSTR-2A FetchMismatch EngineITC Risk Report
⚡ Gap in Standard ERPs

Standard ERPs generate your own GST reports but do not cross-check supplier filing status. This requires GSTN API integration not present in standard systems.

R3Cash vs System Sales Reconciliation▼
📋 About

At day end, compares expected cash (based on all bills) with what the cashier submits. Any gap is flagged with specific transactions that may explain it. 56% of retail theft comes from within — real-time reconciliation reduces this significantly.

⚙️ Logic & Flow
All cash bills summed→Expected drawer amount calculated→Cashier submits physical count→Gap identified→Suspect transactions highlighted
🧩 Key Elements
Expected Cash CalcPhysical Count EntryGap AnalysisTransaction Drill-Down
⚡ Gap in Standard ERPs

Standard ERPs do basic day-end cash entry but don't perform intelligent gap analysis linking missing amounts to specific suspicious transactions.

R4Slow-Moving Stock Velocity Scoring▼
📋 About

Each product gets a velocity score based on units sold per week over 12 weeks. Products below threshold are ranked by money locked in them. The average shop has 18–24% of inventory as slow-moving at any time — velocity scoring is far more actionable than a binary flag.

⚙️ Logic & Flow
12-week rolling sales per SKU→Weekly velocity = units/week→Score assigned (high/med/low)→Ranked by locked capital→Action list generated
🧩 Key Elements
Velocity CalculatorRolling Window (12wk)Capital Lock ReportLiquidation Priority List
⚡ Gap in Standard ERPs

Most ERPs flag items not sold in X days — a simple binary. Velocity scoring is a continuous measure that's far more actionable for prioritizing clearance efforts.

R17Customer Acquisition vs Retention Split▼
📋 About

Every month, shows how many sales came from new customers vs returning ones, and compares average bill value of each group. Acquiring a new customer costs 5–7× more than selling to an existing one. This metric tells you before the problem becomes severe.

⚙️ Logic & Flow
Each bill checked: first-time or returning customer→New/returning flag applied→Monthly split % calculated→Average bill value per group→Trend shown month-on-month
🧩 Key Elements
New/Returning FlagSplit % ReportAvg Bill ComparisonMonth-on-Month Trend
⚡ Gap in Standard ERPs

Requires tagging every bill as new or returning customer — a simple flag that most systems don't implement — and then surfacing it as a meaningful trend report.
